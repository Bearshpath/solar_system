Our Solar System

A brief tour from the center outwards.

☀️ The Sun

The star at the center, containing 99.8% of the Solar System's mass.

The Planets

🌑 Mercury

The smallest planet and closest to the Sun. A year on Mercury is just 88 Earth days.

🟠 Venus

Spins backwards compared to most planets. Hottest planet due to a runaway greenhouse effect.

🌍 Earth

Our home. The only known planet with liquid water on its surface and known life.

🔴 Mars

The "Red Planet". Has the largest volcano (Olympus Mons) and canyon in the Solar System.

🌕 Jupiter

The largest planet. A gas giant with a Great Red Spot, a storm larger than Earth.

🪐 Saturn

Known for its stunning ring system, made mostly of ice particles with some rock debris.

🔵 Uranus

An ice giant that rotates on its side, likely due to a past massive collision.

🔵 Neptune

The most distant planet. A dark, cold ice giant with the fastest winds in the Solar System.

Beyond

🟤 Pluto

A dwarf planet in the Kuiper Belt. Has a large moon named Charon, nearly its own size.
